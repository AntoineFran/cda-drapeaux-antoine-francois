cda-flags

instructions:

Cda-flags is a quizz of flag guessing! The goal of this game is to guess as many and as fast as possible the flag of the countries displayed at the top of the page. You'll have to make match the colors of the pattern under the country name with the colors of its flag by clicking on the different area of the pattern.
As you can see on the exemple above, the game has different levels, a chrono, and count the number of clicks you made to guess a flag country.
Good luck and have fun with flags!
