cda-flags:

instructions:

Cda-flags is a quizz of flag guessing! The goal of this game is easy, there is the name of a country displayed at the top of the page and you have to reconstruct its flag. To succeed in your mission you'll have to click on the different area of the flag to change their color and make them match with the flag of the country requested. Once you did find the flag requested, the next country will appear directly and you'll have to find its flag. As you can see on the exemple below the game has different level, a chrono, and count the number of clicks you used to guess a flag.
